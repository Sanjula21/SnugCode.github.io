Introduction

This is a website for a fictional company called "The Book Club". The Book Club is a company that sells books online. The website allows users to register and login to their account. Once logged in, users can view their account details, view their order history and view their shopping cart. Users can also search for books and add them to their shopping cart. The website also allows users to checkout and pay for their order.
